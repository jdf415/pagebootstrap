How Does Bail Work?

Bail is very easy and very simple to understand. The court and the jail require you to put up money for you or your loved one in jail. The court will hang on to that money until this case is over with. If you do not have the money to be put up for your bail you hire a bail company to put up the money for your or your loved ones release. What we charge you as long as the person whom gets released returns to court is 10% non-refundable fee.
